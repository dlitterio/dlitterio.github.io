About Smart Yeti

My Role: Redesign existing website delivering desktop, tablet, and mobile mockups.

Created at: DESIGNATION Labs

Smart Yeti Studios is a video production studio offering marketing videos and photography for small businesses.

Style Direction

After research and competetive analysis, I came up with some style tiles for design direction. The owner wanted his site to have a smart and approachable feel while providing the content needed to inform the visitors of his products. In my designs, I wanted the user to feel engaged and lead to the information. Since videography is a very dynamic medium, I thought the use of negative space to create a flow pattern would really capture that dynamic scrolling through the site.
