More behind the man

I am a former photographer now UI designer. Earning a BFA in Commercial Arts and a background in photography, I have developed an eye for beautiful things. That could be anything from photographs to interfaces...and even people. While I love photography, I wanted to get into designing something that can be seen and appreciated by people everyday. After diving into the User Centered Design way of thinking, a successful interface should be visually connected with its content leaving the user satisfied. I have worked on the development of branding, marketing page strategy and design, along with mobile app design all in a user centric design approach.
