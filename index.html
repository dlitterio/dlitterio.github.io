More behind the man

I am a former photographer, athlete, and coach. Earning a BFA in Commercial Arts and a background in photography, I have developed an eye for beautiful things. That could be anything from photographs to interfaces...and even people. In the summer of 2015, I attended DESIGNATION, a full stack UX/UI design program learning the ins and outs of User Centered design. While the UCD was aimed toward digital design, after working as an art director intern, the principles apply to more than just design.

I live outside of Chicago, so when I'm not designing you can find me watching the Chicago Blackhawks winning Stanley Cups. I actually don't mind the winters...maybe that's because I'm December born.
