About Panduit

Panduit is a global manufacturer of physical infrastructure equipment that support power, communications, computing, control, and security systems.

The Challenge

My contract was to provide hi-fidelity mockups of a new website navigation menu for their website redesign. I was provided a brand guideline book and wireframes from the client.

The Solution

The solution for the redesign follows brand guidelines including colors and fonts that provides an overall cohesive fit. Along with the provided wireframes, I asked for screenshots of the redesigned homepage to use as a background for my mockups. However, Panduit has not yet launched their new website design.

Before & After

Iconography

The icons used in the design are my own. I created them in Sketch using shape layers. I chose a monochrome set of colors using the teal from the style's color palette. Keeping up to date, I wanted the camera icons to portray the equipment that Smart Yeti uses, which are DSLR cameras.
